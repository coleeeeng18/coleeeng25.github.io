Who Am I

coleng in a nutshell

Ola! I'm a student and a web developer. I serve as a secretary of the "Letran Information Technology Society" for the year 2018-2019. Writing was my first love; I started writing romance novels at the age of 14 and posted it online. My first story entitled, "Tell Me Your Name", which I wrote out of boredom, has already reached 28.3k reads. Being an aspiring author in the future has always been my dream and passion, whereas I could influence and inspire people through my words. But despite my love for writing, I chose a different path and focused in the field of Information Technology instead. I wanted to try new stuff and I wanted to be challenged. Though, programming wasn't really my forte nor have a tiny knowledge about it, however, I grew a little interest in it until it became a hobby. Because of my eagerness to learn, I practiced how to code all by myself with the help of W3school, stack overflow, and some youtube tutorials. I attended various seminars, bootcamps, and tech conferences as an extension of learning aside from the knowledge I'm gaining inside the campus. Now, I'm part of the community, "Women Who Code - Manila". This community has taught me a lot; I learned technically, from the experiences of the successful woman in the IT industry. It motivated me to strive harder and to develop and innovate something that I'm passionate about. Lastly, yes, I'm also a Microsoft Technology Associate in Networking Fundamentals and Windows Server Administration Fundamentals.


Interests

Things I love and passionate about

Yes, I love foods! There's something about foods that I really love; it's my happy pill, my stress reliever, and my love language. Though, I don't usually eat a lot because I've set limits on how many foods I must consume in a day but whenever I get the chance to eat whatever I want, I do eat a lot.



If there's one thing I can do for hours, that would be watching. I've spent countless of hours watching random videos on youtube while lying on a couch and I have to tell you, I won't get tired of it. Whenever I wanted to rest after days of being busy, watching is definitely my way to take a break from everything.



Buzzfeed Unsolved! Yes people, I'm a huuuuge fan. I'm not really into romantic films, drama, etc., but then I discovered this series then suddenly, I fell in love with it. From True Crime to Supernatural, I think I've watched all of its episodes. You'll definitely love Shane and Ryan as you watch their journey in solving crimes and ghoul hunting. I'm encouraging everyone to watch this series (btw, they're on youtube so they're easy to find) because its content is very captivating and pretty fun to watch. Without even knowing it, you're becoming conspiracy theory enthusiast because of this.



You must be thinking, "donuts?". Actually, everyone who knew me so well definitely knows how obsessed I am with donuts. I don't know why or how but I just love them so much. Whenever I'm upset or mad at people, they know how to handle it and give me donuts to make me calm. I've received countless of donuts as a gift from different people even though I didn't asked them to. Anyways, donut is the best thang ever happened in this world. We must be grateful to whoever invented it, he's a life saver.



Do I still have to explain? Lol, kidding. I sing a lot and my friends knows it. May it be on karaoke and while taking a shower. I have this habit of recording my own cover of a song together with its instrumental version. I share them online too but the thing is, the video only last for 15 seconds.



I already mentioned before that writing was my passion, right? Well, I barely write short stories, poems, open letter, etc. nowadays unlike before. The reason why I love it because I can express myself and channel my emotions through words. When I was 14, that's when I collaborated with other authors for an idea for my stories. I was considered a critique, and got my works recognized by other writers as well. I kind of stopped when I got heartbroken because I was so broken that I wrote open letters for almost every day until I ran out of words to describe my train of thoughts.



Pink. Gosh, I'm so in love with pink. My friends knows it and they're all aware that most of my stuff are pink. My bag, pouch, etc., they're all pink. It's my happy color and it perfectly describes my personality. It's sweet, "cute" lol, romantic, very feminine, you name it.



I really love the ocean and the sunset. It's like the most mesmerizing view ever. Whenever I'm in a beach or seaside, I'm always looking forward to watch the sunset after a very long day because it makes me feel that no matter what happened in your day, it will still end as beautiful as the sunset. It makes me feel that life is just so good to see the such beauty; peaceful and lovely.



Lastly, learning. Learning comes in various ways and it doesn't only come in studying. You can learn new stuff from watching documentary series, by reading an article that you're not familiar with, and many more. I love to explore new stuff because life's too short to be stuck with something that you already know. It's fun to learn other things as well, so go explore while you still can. Life has so much more to offer and it would be fun if you're willing to explore them all.
